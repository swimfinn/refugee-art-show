We are three ESL teachers who are passionate about teaching as well as art while wanting to give back to the community. With the way things are going in the world and with the influx of refugees coming into the US, we thought it would be a great idea to offer art classes for refugees who are already professional artists or who would like to take their art to the next level.

Our online course is made for refugees who would like to enter the art world or have a chance to share their story or passion through art. There are 6 classes where you will learn about taking part in art shows, publishing books, art marketing, grant writing, and seeing how art can be therapeutic. At the end of the program, refugees can show their artwork in a gallery and, if they want, talk to visitors and potential buyers about their work.

This hands-on experience helps refugees learn key skills for the art industry and build confidence and community through creativity. It also gives the local community a chance to understand more about the experiences of refugees, helping to build stronger connections and clear up any misconceptions.
