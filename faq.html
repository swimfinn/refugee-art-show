- How much do the classes cost?

__Free


- How much are submission costs?

__Free


- How much is the gallery commission?

__The gallery collects 20%. That is 15% - 20% lower than the average gallery. This is quite the deal!


- Why do I need to pay a commission?

__It's to cover the insurance and labeling costs


- Is this for adults only?

__No, high school students are welcome to participate as well.


- Do I have to take the class to be able to participate?

__Yes, you must take 4 of the 6 classes to participate in the art show.


- Do I have to ship my art to the gallery?

__ It's preferred, but we will have an art pickup in Seattle, Spokane, Richland, and Wenatchee. If you are in the Yakima or Ellensburg area, you can simply drop it off at the gallery.


- I'm not an artist, but a family member in my homecountry is. Can they participate?

__ With the advancement of technology, they might be able to. It will depend on the medium. Please contact the lead on this project, Eileen Finn, to see if it's possible


- Will the class be in English?

__ Yes, but we will be recording the classes, and we will use technology to create subtitles in your language, which we will then upload to a website for you to view. There will be a small quiz at the end of each class to make sure the material was understood.
